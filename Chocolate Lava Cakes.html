Chocolate Lava Cakes

 

Ingredients:

1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter

4 ounces (about 113g) semi-sweet or bittersweet chocolate, chopped

2 large eggs

2 large egg yolks

1/4 cup granulated sugar

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Pinch of salt

Optional: Powdered sugar, vanilla ice cream, or berries for serving

Instructions:

Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and lightly flour four ramekins.

Melt Chocolate and Butter: In a heatproof bowl, melt the butter and chopped chocolate together. You can do this by placing the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) or by melting in the microwave in 20-second intervals, stirring between each interval until smooth. Allow the mixture to cool slightly.

Prepare Batter: In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla extract until well combined. Add the melted chocolate and butter mixture, and stir until smooth.

Incorporate Dry Ingredients: Sift in the flour and a pinch of salt, then gently fold the dry ingredients into the chocolate mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

Fill Ramekins: Divide the batter equally among the prepared ramekins.

Bake: Place the filled ramekins on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 12-14 minutes. The edges should be set, but the center should still be soft.

Serve: Carefully remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool for a minute. Run a knife around the edges, then invert each cake onto a serving plate. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or berries if desired.

These Chocolate Lava Cakes are a delightful treat with a gooey, molten center that's sure to impress!
