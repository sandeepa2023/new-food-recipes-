Caprese Skewers

 

Ingredients:

Cherry tomatoes

Fresh mozzarella balls (bocconcini)

Fresh basil leaves

Balsamic glaze

Sea salt (optional)

Instructions:

Prepare the Ingredients: Gather the cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella balls, and fresh basil leaves. If the mozzarella balls are large, you can cut them into smaller, bite-sized pieces.

Assemble the Skewers: Take small skewers or toothpicks and begin assembling the skewers. Start by threading a cherry tomato onto the skewer, followed by a folded basil leaf, and then a mozzarella ball. Repeat this pattern until the skewer is filled, ending with a cherry tomato on top.

Arrange and Drizzle: Place the assembled Caprese skewers on a serving platter. Before serving, drizzle balsamic glaze over the skewers. The glaze adds a sweet and tangy flavor that complements the freshness of the tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.

Optional: Sprinkle Sea Salt: If desired, you can sprinkle a small amount of sea salt over the skewers just before serving. This enhances the flavors and adds a bit of extra depth.

Serve: Arrange the Caprese skewers on a platter and serve them as a delightful and elegant appetizer. These skewers are perfect for parties, gatherings, or as a light and refreshing snack.

Enjoy your homemade Caprese Skewers!
